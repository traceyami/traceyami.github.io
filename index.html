About

I build products by starting with people, not features. My path to product leadership wasn't linear—I learned how to listen in retail, how to build community through events, and how to solve real problems in customer service. Those experiences shaped how I think about design: always human-first, always questioning assumptions.

Today I lead product and design, but I still get my hands dirty. I believe the best work happens when strategy and craft aren't separated.
